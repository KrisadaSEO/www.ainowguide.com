# One Mouse Marketing  -  Constellation Manifesto

**Position:** Digital trade skills for the AI age.

## What this constellation is

The personal ecosystem. This is where Krisada teaches what Krisada does  -  SEO, AI-directed content, website ownership, digital monetization  -  to an audience that wants to build digital income without a personal brand, social media presence, or public-facing identity.

The Quiet Marketer thesis lives here. The AI Director identity lives here. The brand tagline lives here.

`krisada.com` is the hub. `1mousemarketing.com` is the guild. `digitaltradeschools.com` is the education brand.

## The audience

People who want to generate digital income by building systems rather than performing for cameras. The "lab scientist in the trenches" audience rather than the influencer audience.
