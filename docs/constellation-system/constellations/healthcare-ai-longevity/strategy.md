1. `agebettertoday.com`  -  strongest consumer intent, least YMYL scrutiny; start here
2. `longevityforward.com`  -  longevity science content; pairs with agebettertoday.com
3. `aihealthcarenow.com`  -  flagship AI healthcare hub; needs real expertise signals
4. `naturalherblibrary.com`  -  content library model; pairs well with affiliate revenue
5. `digitalhealthins.com` / `onlinehealthins.com`  -  insurance comparison; high CPC affiliate
6. `lymeorg.com`  -  specialist authority; requires deep topical commitment

- Affiliate (supplements, health products, insurance): highest near-term revenue
- Display advertising: high RPM in health vertical
- Health insurance lead gen: very high value per lead
- Telehealth / practitioner referrals
