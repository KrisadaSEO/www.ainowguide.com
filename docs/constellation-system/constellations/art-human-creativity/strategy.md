# Art & Human Creativity  -  Strategy

## Build sequence

1. `krisadaart.com`  -  personal art brand; activate as portfolio/gallery
2. `buyhumanart.com`  -  high-concept brand; content + marketplace
3. `einkcollections.com`  -  early position in e-ink art vertical
4. `newartegallery.com`  -  gallery/marketplace hub
5. `digitalartistlife.com`  -  education content for digital artists
6. Print cluster (`fineinkprint.com`, `artprintsnposters.com`)  -  POD affiliate or fulfillment

## Monetization paths

- Print-on-demand: Printful/Printify affiliate on artprintsnposters.com
- Original art sales: krisadaart.com, buyartweb.com
- Gallery commission: newartegallery.com
- E-ink display affiliate: einkcollections.com
- Artist education: digitalartistlife.com, aiartistlife.com

## Cautions

- Don't build too many marketplace/gallery domains without a real inventory strategy
- The art market requires ongoing curation  -  this constellation is higher-maintenance than content plays
- `eartcollections.com`, `artdecorlife.com`, `einkgallery.com`, `fineinkprints.com` are variants of stronger primaries; redirect rather than build separately
