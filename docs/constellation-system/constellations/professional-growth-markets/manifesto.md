# Professional Growth Markets  -  Constellation Manifesto

**Position:** Industry-specific marketing ecosystems  -  lead generation properties for defined verticals.

## What this constellation is

Seven distinct vertical markets, each with its own domain cluster. These are B2B lead generation plays  -  connecting service providers (medical practices, spas, manufacturers) with marketing expertise.

The naming pattern (seomarketingdr.com, medmarketingdr.com) creates a recognizable family across the medical vertical. The spa cluster mirrors this. The industrial and technology plays (flexpackmfg.com, as400ibmsystem.com) are high-specificity niche bets with low competition.

## The AS/400 opportunity

`as400ibmsystem.com` / `as400ibmsystems.com` are sleeper assets. There are thousands of mid-size manufacturers still running IBM AS/400 / iSeries systems. The people who maintain them are aging out of the workforce. The search volume is niche but the intent is extremely high  -  and competition is minimal.

## Flagship

`medmarketingdr.com`  -  the medical marketing hub; bridges to c5.

- `medmarketingdr.com` → c5 (healthcare marketing lives in both verticals)
- `pharmdigitalmarketing.com` → c5 (pharma is healthcare)
