# AI Digital Karma  -  Strategy

## Build sequence

1. **`aidigitalkarma.com`**  -  flagship content site first. Publishes the framework, earns the brand authority.
2. **`ainowguide.com`**  -  AI readiness guide for businesses. High search intent, clear audience.
3. **`aisymantics.com`**  -  semantic AI framework brand. Authority content on structured data and entity SEO.
4. **`datasetseo.com`**  -  the crossover asset. Bridges AI infrastructure content with the digital asset investing thesis.
5. **`aidataexchangecom.com`**  -  longer-term build. Requires a real product or marketplace concept to activate.
6. **ai-to-ai network** (`aitoai*.com` cluster)  -  hold as infrastructure naming assets. Activate when the agent-to-agent use case matures.

## Monetization paths

- **Content + affiliate**  -  aidigitalkarma.com, ainowguide.com as authority publications with AI tool affiliate revenue
- **Consulting / productized service**  -  AI readiness audits, structured data implementation, dataset builds
- **Dataset licensing**  -  datasetsbuilder.com, datasetsmaker.com as dataset production brands
- **Domain sales**  -  the aitoai* cluster has strong naming value as the agent ecosystem grows

## Priority actions

- [ ] Launch aidigitalkarma.com as a content site

- [ ] Build ainowguide.com as a lead-gen property
- [ ] Decide: build aisymantics.com as a standalone brand or fold into aidigitalkarma.com

## Cautions

- The aitoai* cluster is timing-dependent  -  the agent-to-agent market isn't mature enough to build content into yet
- Don't over-build supporting variants (aisymantix.com, symatixai.com) before the primary brand is established
