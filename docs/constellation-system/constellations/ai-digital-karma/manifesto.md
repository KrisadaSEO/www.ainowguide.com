# AI Digital Karma  -  Constellation Manifesto

**Position:** Building the AI-readable web.

## What this constellation is

AI Digital Karma is the infrastructure layer of the Krisada portfolio.

It's built on a core insight: as AI systems become the primary discovery mechanism for content, products, and services, the web needs a new kind of trust and readability layer. Not just technical SEO. Not just backlinks. A deeper structural signal that says  -  this site, this brand, this entity was *built for* AI to understand.

That's AI Digital Karma.

The karma isn't mystical. It's structural. It's what you earn when:
- Your data is clean and machine-readable
- Your entity relationships are explicit
- Your datasets are shareable and useful to AI training pipelines
- Your site architecture signals trust at the schema level

## Why this exists

The web was built for humans to read. Search engines built crawlers to approximate what humans value. AI systems need something different  -  they need *structured truth*, not keyword density.

The domains in this constellation are building the vocabulary, infrastructure, and frameworks for that transition.

## The flagship

`aidigitalkarma.com`  -  the brand anchor and public explanation layer for the concept.

## Key concepts

- **AI readability**  -  structuring content so AI systems can parse, trust, and cite it
- **Dataset SEO**  -  building datasets as SEO assets, not just pages
- **Agent-to-agent infrastructure**  -  the plumbing for AI systems talking to each other
- **Symantics**  -  the branded semantic AI framework emerging from aisymantics.com

## Crossover bridges

This constellation feeds **c3 Digital Asset Investing** via `datasetseo.com` (datasets as investable assets) and **c2 AI Website Systems** via `aitoaiweb.com` / `aitoaiwebsites.com` (the infrastructure that powers automated site production).
