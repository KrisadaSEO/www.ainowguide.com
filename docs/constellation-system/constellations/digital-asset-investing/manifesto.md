# Digital Asset Investing  -  Constellation Manifesto

**Position:** Digital assets as an investment class.

## What this constellation is

The highest-value business cluster in the portfolio. This is where website acquisitions, digital property valuation, and the thesis of the internet as investable infrastructure all live.

The frame is simple: just as real estate investors buy, improve, and sell physical properties, digital asset investors do the same with websites, domain portfolios, and online income streams.

## Why this matters now

AI is collapsing the cost of creating web content. That means:
1. Low-quality sites get devalued fast
2. Well-positioned authority sites become more scarce and valuable
3. The skill of *identifying* undervalued digital assets becomes a real edge

This constellation teaches and monetizes that edge.
