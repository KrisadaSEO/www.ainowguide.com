# Digital Asset Investing  -  Strategy

## Build sequence

1. `buyseowebsites.com`  -  strongest commercial intent; build first as a directory or marketplace
2. `smartdigitalinvesting.com`  -  flagship content hub for the investment thesis
3. `digitalpropertystore.com`  -  commerce/listings play
4. `aiassetleverage.com`  -  AI angle on asset leverage; connects to c1 story

## Monetization paths

- Marketplace/brokerage: listing fee or commission on website sales
- Lead gen: connecting buyers/sellers of digital properties
- Education: course on buying and valuing SEO websites
- Affiliate: Flippa, Empire Flippers, hosting affiliates

## Cautions

- `shopdigitalproperty.com`, `assetclassleverage.com`, `leveragebuilder.com` are supporting variants  -  don't build until primary brands are established
- Requires ongoing content to maintain authority; not a set-and-forget play
